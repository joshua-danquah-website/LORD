General Guidelines

At Coptal, we're committed to maintaining a professional and respectful environment for all users. These guidelines help ensure a positive experience for everyone.

  • Be respectful and professional in all interactions
  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Respect others' privacy and confidentiality
  • Report any violations or suspicious activity

Job Posting Guidelines

For Employers

  • Provide accurate job descriptions
  • Include clear requirements and responsibilities
  • Specify location and work arrangement (remote/hybrid/onsite)
  • Be transparent about compensation and benefits
  • Respond to applications in a timely manner

Profile Guidelines

For Job Seekers

  • Use a professional profile photo
  • Provide accurate work history and qualifications
  • Keep your profile information up to date
  • Be honest about your skills and experience

Communication Guidelines

  • Maintain professional communication at all times
  • Respond to messages promptly
  • Be clear and concise in your communications
  • Report any harassment or inappropriate behavior

Privacy Guidelines

  • Protect your personal information
  • Don't share confidential company information
  • Respect data protection regulations
  • Review our privacy policy for detailed information
