Frequently Asked Questions

Account Management

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign up" button in the top right corner and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password.

How can I reset my password?

Click "Log in", then select "Forgot Password". Enter your email address to receive password reset instructions.

Job Search

How do I search for specific jobs?

Use the search bar on the homepage to enter keywords, job titles, or company names. You can also use filters to narrow down your search.

Can I save job searches?

Yes, you can save searches and receive email alerts when new matching jobs are posted. You'll need to be logged in to use this feature.
